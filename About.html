Company History

In the spring of 2005, a passionate group of explorers returned from a hiking trip through the Balkans, inspired by the rich cultures, untold stories, and hidden gems they had encountered. Fueled by a desire to share these magical experiences with others, they founded Aurora Horizons Travel Co.—a boutique tourism company dedicated to crafting unforgettable journeys. What started as a small team offering walking tours through Serbia soon expanded into a trusted name in adventure and cultural tourism across Europe and North Africa. By 2010, Aurora Horizons had launched its signature “Legends & Landscapes” series, blending historical storytelling with immersive local experiences.
